About the Workshop

Scope

The 3rd International Workshop on Adaptive, Compressive and Computational Imaging WACCI 2024 covers the most recent and exciting advances in state-of-the-art computational imaging topics, with plenary speakers, and special sessions. In addition, several matchmaking events targeting students and professionals will be featured in WACCI 2024.

Topics of Interest

Compressive sensing - Unconventional imaging modalities
Imaging at extreme scales - 3D Imaging - Superresolution
Lensless imaging - Deep learning for computational imaging
Imaging through turbid media - Spectral imaging
Phase retrieval - Wavefront sensing - Adaptive optics
Inverse problems - Fourier Ptychography
